# Activity Sheets
Several types of activities fall into the category of _activity sheets;_ printed out papers that contain activities in and of themselves. For these, students can be given writing utensils and the printed sheets. There can be prizes for students who complete the sheets.

## Rebus Puzzles
[Click here for the folder](./Assets/RebusPuzzles/)

These are simple word puzzles. There are several sheets, although only some of them have answers.

## Where's Waldo
[Click here for the PDF](./Assets/WhereIsWaldo.pdf)

This is classic [Where's Waldo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Where%27s_Wally%3F). Print out sheets and have students circle Waldo.

## Nonograms
[Click here for the PDF](./Assets/NonogramsSheet.pdf)

These are [a type of logic puzzle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nonogram). The PDF includes instructions, but it may be better to print out only a range of pages for everyone (and just a couple copies of the instructions / answers).
